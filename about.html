ABOUT US

Welcome to African DELICACIES, where every meal tells the story of Africa's rich culture and heritage.
We serve authentic traditional dishes prepared with love, locally sourced ingredients,and time-honored recipes passed down through generations.
Whether you crave spicy stews, tender meats, or flavorful vegetaranian options, you'll always find a taste of home here.

THE STORY

African Delicacies began as a simple dream to bring the true taste of African culture to every table.
Founded in 2025 in Nairobi Kenya, the restaurant started as a small family kitchen serving home style meals inspired by traditional recipes from different African regions.
What started as a humble eatery quickly gained popularity for its rich flavors,warm hospitality, and authentic touch.
The founders believed that food is not just nourishment but a celebration of identity a way of keeping African traditions alive through every bite.
Over the years, African Delicacies has grown from a local favorite into a symbol of African pride and unity.
Each meal tells a story from the spicy jollof rice of West Africa to the flavorful ugali and nyama choma of East Africa, and the aromatic curries of the South.
Today, the restaurant continues to blend the old and new
maintaining the soul of traditional cooking while embracing modern culinary creativity
